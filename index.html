Country I Come From

I was born in Rwanda, a small country in East Africa, mostly known as "A thousand Hills Country". Rwanda is surrounded by Uganda in North, Tanzania in West, Burundi in South and D.R.Congo in East. Rwanda is a small country of 12 millions people on an area of 26,338 sq km *(7 times smaller that Washington!)*.
Rwanda has a temperate tropical highland climate, with lower temperatures than are typical for equatorial countries due to its high elevation.
In Rwanda, we speak 4 languages: Kinyarwanda, English, French and Swahili. (I am learning English, but i do speak fluently the 3 other languages!).
